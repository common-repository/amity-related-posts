Amity Related Posts Lite is a highly customizable WordPress plugin that linking to related posts from your website/blog archive.

== Description ==

Amity Related Posts Lite is a WordPress Plugin that appears under each post, linking to related posts from your website/blog archive. It helps you to show other articles in the same category.

Check official website for live demo: [Click Here](http://arp.amitytheme.com/amity-related-posts-plugin/)


= Keep Readers Engaged, Increase your page views =

The plugin links to posts that are relevant and interesting to readers of a particular post, keeping them engaged with your blog, and increasing your traffic.

= Elegant and Responsive Design =

Amity Related Posts Lite is minimally styled and responsive design.

= Main Features: =
* Advanced Options Panel
* Responsive Layout
* Grid view and List view

== Installation ==

= From your WordPress dashboard =

1. Visit **Plugins > Add New**
2. Search for **Amity Related Posts Lite**
3. Activate **Amity Related Posts** from your Plugins page.

= From FTP =

1. Download **Amity Related Posts Lite** Zip Folder.
2. Upload the **Amity Related Posts** zip folder to your **/wp-content/plugins/** directory.
3. Activate **Amity Related Posts Lite** from your Plugins page.
